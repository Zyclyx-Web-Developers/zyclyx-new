Implementing IT Governance to ensure regulatory compliance

Compliance Assessment

A compliance assessment is an analysis of discrepancies. We look for differences to be found between your current control system and what is required. It is not a risk assessment, and the gaps identified may or may not correlate with exposure to risk.

Vulnerability Assessment / Pen Test / RED

Vulnerability assessment is the practice of defining, detecting, classifying and prioritizing vulnerabilities in computer systems, software and network infrastructure and providing the enterprise with the required knowledge, understanding and risk history to recognize and react appropriately to the threats to their environment. Pen Test - Pen test is termed as penetration test, which is a simulated cyber attack on the computer system to check for bugs that can be exploited.

SOC Automation

It is a facility that contains an information security team in charge of constantly monitoring and evaluating the security posture of an entity. The main goal is to identify, evaluate, and respond to cyber-security events by combining technology solutions with a strong set of processes.

Implement (SIEM)

Security Information and Event Management (SIEM) enables you to get real-time analysis of the threats and security warnings generated by network and hardware applications. It manages the storage, processing, analysing and reporting of various security data and allows you to compare different events and warnings.

Forensic Services / Incident Response

Forensic services is a process of investigating, detecting, documenting, collecting, processing, analyzing and presenting computer-related evidence in support of network vulnerability mitigation and/or criminal, fraud, counterintelligence, or law enforcement investigations. Incident response is a coordinated approach to handling and managing the consequences of a security breach or cyber-attack, also known as an incident involving IT, computer accident or defense. The goal is to treat the situation in a way that limits harm and reduces the time and cost of recovery
